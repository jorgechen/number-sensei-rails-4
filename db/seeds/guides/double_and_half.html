Method

This simple trick is what it says: double one number, half the other. Do this to make the multiplication easier.

  • Check if one number can be made into an easier-to-multiply number. For example, 15 can be doubled to be 30.
  • Double one number. Halve the other number.
  • Multiply for the answer.

Roman Numerals are a different way of writing numbers. People often use them to make names look cooler, like King James VI (the 6th) or Super Bowl XLV (45).

To figure out a Roman numeral, just add up all the values from each symbol. However, when a smaller symbol is before a bigger symbol, the smaller symbol should be subtracted from the larger symbol. Examples: IV = 4, XIV = 14, CXCV = 195

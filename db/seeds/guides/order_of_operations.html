To tackle questions with different operations, we must follow a set of rules called the order of operations.

We coan remember the order of operations with this catchy phrase: Please Excuse My Dear Aunt Sally.

    Each letter represents an operation:
  1. P – parenthesis, from the innermost to the outermost
  2. E – exponents, from the innermost to the outermost
  3. M – multiplication, D – division, from left to right
  4. A – addition, S – subtraction, from left to right

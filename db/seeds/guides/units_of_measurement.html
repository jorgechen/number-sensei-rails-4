Don't be that kid who doesn't know how many cups are in a gallon. Don't live your life not knowing these units of measurement!

Distance
1 m = 1000 mm 1 ft = 12 in
1 m = 100 cm 1 yd = 3 ft
1 km = 1000 m 1 mi = 5280 ft
1 in = 2.54 cm 1 mi = 1760 yd
Volume
1 gallon = 4 quarts 1 gallon = 231 in3
1 quart = 2 pints 1 tbsp (table spoon) = 3 tsp (tea spoons)
1 pint = 2 cups 1 L = 1000 mL
1 cup = 8 oz. 1 cup = 16 tbsp
Weight
1 g = 1000 mg 1 lb = 8 oz
1 kg = 1000 g 1 ton = 2000 lbs
